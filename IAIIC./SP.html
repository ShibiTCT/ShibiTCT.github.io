sub-projects Give a dog a sign (GADAS) GADAS is a sub-project of CATE which has an aim of distributing as many assistance dogs only signs as possible to replace old and outdated signs such as 'guide dogs only' and 'registered assistance dogs only'. This project requires the aid of the general public to distribute signs and information sheets. The Grand Assistance Dog Survey (TGADS) TGADS is a sub-project in developement and is ment to survey both organisations and individuals to help understand and approximate how many assistance dogs are present within the UK and what their demographic looks like.
