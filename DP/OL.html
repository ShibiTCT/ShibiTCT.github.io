Please be aware that some organisations listed are not assistance dog organisations but are dog trainers or dog training organisations which have a specific programme for assistance dogs, eg. membership .

To go to the organisations website please click on the name. We can not confirm the legitmacy of any organisation listed, please always do your own research into these organisations.
