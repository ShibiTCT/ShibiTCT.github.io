Welcome to CATE!!

This section is targeted toward Disabled people who are looking for an assistance dog. To change what section you are in, hover over the I am... button to the right of the logo and a drop down box will allow you to pick options. Even if you are not in the section target demographic, the infomation may still be useful to you.
